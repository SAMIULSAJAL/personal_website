Thesis: Formation and Oxidation of Nano Transition Metals

Title: Formation and oxidation of nano transition metals in the laser ablation technique by the ReaxFF reactive force field using Molecular Dynamics in High-Performance Computers

DOI: 10.13140/RG.2.2.21838.43845

Description: Molecular Dynamics simulations were performed to investigate phase transitions of bulk aluminum in aqueous environments using ReaxFF force fields on HPC. We studied the RMSD of nano-clusters, formation rates, sizes, and thermodynamic properties influencing the oxidation mechanism.
